Welcome!

I created this site to pay homage to a story from the early days of the internet about a user named "TimeTravel_0," or more commonly known as John Titor

In the years between 1998-2001, Titor made several posts to online forums insisting that he was a time traveler from the year 2036. The contents of these posts ranged from predictions of what would happen in our future, to theories on the existence of multiple worldlines.

Up to his last post in 2001, he made a number of predictions on what would happen in the near future.

Who is he?
